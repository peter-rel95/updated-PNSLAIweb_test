CAPITAL CONTRIBUTION

Dividend rate for CY 2023:12.50% Per Annum.

Refers to the individual amount contributed by the members which form the capital of the Association.

A minimum amount of ₱1,000.00 should be maintained to earn income in the form of dividend. The dividend is computed based on the Lowes Quarterly Balance (LQB) and distributed annually.

A maximum of ₱5,000.00 per month from the payroll/pension of the members can be accepted as deposit.

Apply Now!

SAVINGS DEPOSIT ACCOUNT

A savings product with a minimum initial deposit of ₱100.00 that earns interest at the rate to be determined by the Board of Trustees (BOT) computed based on the Lowest Monthly Balance (LMB) compounded and/or distributed monthly.

Current Interest Rate - 2.50% per annum.

Apply Now!

SPECIAL SAVINGS DEPOSIT ACCOUNT

A saving product earning a higher interest rate than savings deposit account that earns interest at the rate to be determined by the Board of Trustees (BOT) computed based on the Lowest Monthly Balance (LMB) compounded and/or distributed monthly.

A maximum of ₱20,000.00 per month from the payroll/pension of the members can be accepted as deposit.

Current Interest Rate- 6% per annum.
