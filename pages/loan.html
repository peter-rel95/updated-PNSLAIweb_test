  • Loanable Amount - minimum of ₱100,000.00 up to ₱1,000,000.00 maximum loanable amount.

  • Loan Term - 12 up to 60 months.

  • Loanable Amount - shall not exceed 50% of the proceeds of his/her commutation of unused leave credits.

  • Loan Term - 12 months only.

  • Add-on Interest Rate per annum - 0.58% monthly or 7% per annum effective October 15, 2021

  • Effective Interest Rate (EIR) per annum - 8.35%

  • Effectivity Date - October 21, 2021

BACK-TO-BACK LOAN

A loan against capital contribution or deposit account of a regular and honorary member.

  • Eligibility - Regular and/or associate members of the Association as enumerated in the By-Laws to include the qualified PCG personnel are eligible to borrow.

  • Loanable Amount - 85% of the withdrawable balance on Cacpcon Accounts
    and 90% of the withdrawable balance of Deposit Accounts (SDA/SSDA)

  • Loan Term - minimum maturity period of 15 days up to maximum of one(1) year.

  • Add-on Interest Rate per annum - Latest Dividend Rate +2%

  • Effective Interest Rate (EIR) per annum -

    • Capital contribution-15.87%
    • Savings Deposit Account-5.29%
    • Special Savings Deposit Account-8.82%

Requirements:

  • Evidence of CAPCON/SDA/SSDA deposit.

  • Duly accomplished/approved application for loan.

  • Photocopy of 2 valid identification cards.

  • Documentary stamp tax if loan is more than ₱250,000.00.
